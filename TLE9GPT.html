Why do these problems occur?


It may be due to people's doings. Things such as pollution, illegal logging, and waste production that affects every living thing on the environment. Mostly everybody on the environment not only people, but also other living things like animals, plants, and others. But besides of these problems, there is one huge pandemic that everyone is aware of, and that is Covid-19



Is Covid-19 a problem we should be aware of?



Covid-19 is a infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. Most people infected with the COVID-19 virus will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment. Older people, and those with underlying medical problems like cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic respiratory disease, and cancer are more likely to develop serious illness


What range of age can Covid-19 affect?

All age groups can be affected by Covid-19, no matter how old or young the patient is.

-HOW TO AVOID COVID-19-



1.Stay home as much as you can. In this way, you can avoid much contact to people that may have the disease.

2.Keep a safe distance. Maintaining a safe distance is needed and must have at least 5 feet apart from each other.

3.Wash hands often.Being clean at all times helps prevent Covid-19.

4.Wear face masks at all times. This may prevent the contaminated air we breathe from other people.

5.Sick? Visit your doctor. When feeling sick, it is the best to consult to a doctor for further details.

It is a must to follow all these procedures to prevent/avoid Covid-19 from spreading.
